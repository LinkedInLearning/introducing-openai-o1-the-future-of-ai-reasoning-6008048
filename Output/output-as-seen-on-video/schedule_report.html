4. Summary

In this proposed schedule:

  • Each day is covered by a morning shift (8am–2pm) and an afternoon shift (2pm–8pm).
  • The scheduling respects employee availability and individual preferences as provided.
  • Employees with “8‑2” availability (Alice, Eric, George, and Carol on weekends) are used exclusively for morning shifts.
  • Employees with “2‑8” availability (Bob, Carol on Wed–Thu, and Fiona) cover the afternoon slots.
  • The Monday afternoon slot (2pm–8pm) lacks a perfect match; therefore, an exception is made by assigning Dave (whose usual window is 10am–4pm) on Monday afternoons. Staff agreement for this exception is assumed.
  • Due to the discrepancy between their structured times and required shift times, Dave and Hannah (both 10‑4 available) are generally not assigned except for the necessary Monday exception.

This schedule is repeated with slight rotations on the morning side to distribute assignments among Alice, Eric, and George while keeping everyone’s day‐off and preference constraints in mind.

Conclusion

The schedule meets the requirement of having one employee on each shift every day, fully respecting employee availability and preferences where possible. The only noted conflict is the Monday afternoon shift, which is addressed by a one‐time adjustment. Further discussion with Dave may be needed to confirm this adjustment.
